## Short description / promotional text

Inspect folders and archives from Finder Quick Look without opening them first.

## Description

FolderPeek extends macOS Quick Look so you can inspect what is inside folders and common archives before opening them.

Select a folder, zip archive, or tar archive in Finder, press Space, and FolderPeek shows a clear contents preview with file names, sizes, modified dates, and thumbnails when available.

FolderPeek is designed to stay lightweight and private:

- No background indexing
- No preview history
- No account required
- No analytics or tracking
- No archive extraction
- No extra macOS permissions required

Use it when you have many folders or archives and need to find the right material quickly without opening each one.

## App Review notes draft
